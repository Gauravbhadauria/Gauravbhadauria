A smart classified ads marketplace built for the UAE market, powered by AI. The platform uses machine learning for auto-categorisation of listings, intelligent price suggestions, and personalised recommendations for buyers. Delivered end-to-end — mobile app, web portal, and a full-featured admin panel.

**Scope:** Mobile App · Web Portal · Backend · Admin Panel  
**Stack:** `React Native` `Next.js` `Node.js` `MongoDB` `AI/ML Integration` `REST API`

---

### 🎵 Music Streaming App

A full-featured music app with artist profiles, album management, playlist creation, offline playback, and social sharing. Built for both iOS and Android with seamless audio streaming, background play, and a clean minimal UI.

**Scope:** Mobile App (iOS + Android) · Backend · Streaming Infrastructure  
**Stack:** `React Native` `Node.js` `Firebase` `Audio Streaming API` `Push Notifications`

---

### 💼 Job Portal — UAE

A comprehensive job platform connecting employers and candidates across the UAE. Features include smart job listings with advanced filters, resume upload, recruiter dashboard, application tracking, and automated job alerts — with a full admin panel for platform management.

**Scope:** Mobile App · Web Portal · Backend · Admin Panel  
**Stack:** `React Native` `React.js` `Node.js` `MongoDB` `Firebase Auth` `Admin Dashboard`

---

### ⚡ EV Bike Booking App

Electric bike booking platform designed for urban commuters. Users locate nearby bikes, book rides, unlock via QR code, track live, and pay in-app. Operators get a dedicated fleet management admin panel with analytics, maintenance logs, and revenue reports.

**Scope:** Mobile App · Backend · Admin Panel  
**Stack:** `React Native` `Live GPS` `Node.js` `Payment Gateway` `Socket.io` `Admin Panel`

---

### 🎬 Trailer Launch & Rating App

A social platform for the film industry where producers officially launch trailers and collect early audience ratings and feedback before release. Includes live voting, audience comments, trending charts, and a producer analytics dashboard to measure pre-release buzz.

**Scope:** Mobile App · Backend · Producer Dashboard  
**Stack:** `React Native` `Firebase` `Video Streaming` `Real-time Ratings` `Node.js`
